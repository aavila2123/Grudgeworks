Notable Brands

House of Errors


"SHOW DON'T TELL. "
A brand with very few words but stunning imagery. London-born brand House of Errors has been making a name for itself as the new face of experimental streetwear. CEO and founder Fully took a new direction in design when he stepped away from architecture and picked up a sewing machine: what started as a DIY project has formed into a successful brand and lifestyle.

Brigade


Established in 2014. Brigade is a unisex lifestyle brand firmly rooted in the raw energy and authenticity of its birthplace, New York. Built on the principle of community over commodity, Brigade works to embody this ethos through every collection where the team collaborates with a diverse array of local models, stylists, musicians, and artists. Brigade remains conscious of sustainability, knowing that things of quality will stand the test of time. More than just a brand, Brigade is a lifestyle, a movement, and a celebration of individuality within a collective spirit.

Esenes WW


Founded by designer Justin Son, ESENES Worldwide is a brand based on self-belief — its name is pronounced “SNS,” shorthand for “sorry not sorry”

Sp5der WW


Founded in 2019, SP5DER is a lifestyle brand based out of Los Angeles by way of Atlanta. The brand is a visual commentary on the pulse of the south. A loud, boisterous sense of self-expression inspired by Atlanta and the community that shapes it.Designed from a luxury perspective with a punk spirit, Sp5der stands at the juxtaposition of nostalgia and invention.

Psychworld


Los Angeles-based lifestyle brand Psychworld first debuted in 2018. Founded by multi-hyphenate DJ and producer Zack Bia, the streetwear brand trades on exclusivity and elusiveness. Psychworld’s unique graphic design and inventive typography style have created a new niche in the highly stratified world of streetwear. Worn by the likes of Drake and Young Thug, the most well known Psychworld hats and short sleeve t-shirts feature a neon slime green logo. Following the Cortiez playbook, the Psychworld Instagram page is often set to private. Word of mouth and eye-catching graphics, not influencer marketing budgets, have popularized Psychworld’s watch and shorts offerings.

Cease and Desist


Cease and Desist is a brand found in the US officially founded by Ghanaian designer Fred Foster in 2014. Foster started his career creating custom pieces for big name artists including Future. That project would evolve into Foster designing merch for Futures pop-ups, events and music label. Foster has a diverse portfolio in the fashion industry with countless projects under his belt varying from design to creative direction.
